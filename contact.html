Stay in Touch

We'd love to hear from prospective students, collaborators, and the press.

Get Involved

We are always looking for motivated students and researchers to join the group as well as collaborations and providing taught opportunities. If you are interested in working with us, please read the sections below before reaching out.

If you want regular updates on our activities, subscribe to our newsletter.

Prospective PhD Students

If you are interested in a PhD position, please email us with your CV, a short research proposal (3 to 5 pages), and any relevant work (papers, code, projects). Note that positions are subject to funding availability.

Collaborations

We welcome collaborations with other academic groups, charities, public organisations, and industry partners. If you have a project idea that aligns with our interests, feel free to reach out.

Taught courses

We are glad to offer educational opportunities to stakeholders who need them. These courses can be discussed to evaluate the best solutions available. If interested, please email us to organise a meeting.

Press & Media

For media enquiries, please contact us by email and we will respond as quickly as possible.
